    A Short Course for Engineers

    ABAQUS is a high-powered finite element analysis (FEA) software package used by industrial and academic researchers throughout the world. This short course is designed to introduce you to the fundamentals of the interface and a variety of modeling techniques through engineering exercises and physical models.

    This course will be offered in April 2015 by Computational Science and Engineering for a fee of $200, intended to recover costs of course development and delivery. Registration will be via Eventbrite and will open up the first week of March.

    Topics

    • Outline & Interface
    • Workflow
    • Postprocessing
    • Job Processing/Solver
    • Meshing & Mesh Conditioning
    • Loading & Analysis
    • Material, Yield, & Fracture Models
    • Section Properties/Composite Objects
    • Dynamic/Contact FEA
    • Batch Programming/Transient Solver
    • Coupling Physics
    • Command Line Interface/Python Scripting
